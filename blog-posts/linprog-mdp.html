Solving Markov Decision Processes Using Linear Programming

Posted on: January 12th, 2025

In this blog post, we will explore how to solve Markov Decision Processes (MDPs) using linear programming techniques. MDPs are mathematical models used for decision-making in situations where outcomes are partly random and partly under the control of a decision maker. By leveraging linear programming, we can find optimal policies that maximize the expected reward over time.
