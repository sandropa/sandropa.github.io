Selected awards from math competitions

In high school, I actively competed in mathematics. While I was okay in algebra, geometry, and number theory, combinatorics was my strongest area and remains a subject I’m passionate about. I enjoy teaching and solving combinatorial problems, particularly in areas like graph theory, algorithms, and enumerative combinatorics. I find these interests closely linked to my current passions in AI and machine learning.
